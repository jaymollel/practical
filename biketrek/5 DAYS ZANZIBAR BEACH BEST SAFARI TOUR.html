Overview

On arrival you will be met and greet by our guide and driver at Zanzibar Airport / Zanzibar Ferry Terminal. Our representative would be holding a name card for you for easy identification and transfer to the Seyyida Hotel/ Similar Accommodation.

Our private car with a professional driver and guide will make your visit to Zanzibar more meaningful and enjoyable. This itinerary allows you to have the chance to discover the best activities in Zanzibar Island including the Zanzibar Spice Tour, Stone Town City Tour, Safari Blue Tour and Prison Island Boat Trip.

DAY 4 - PICKUP FOR ROMANTIC DHOW SUNSET CRUISE
5 days Tanznia zanzibar safari tour

After breakfast, enjoy some free time. In the afternoon, enjoy the world-famous sunset of Zanzibar’s West Coast on a traditional wooden dhow. Sunset dhow cruise in Zanzibar is a wonderful romantic dhow cruise from Stone Town, you will start your journey in a Traditional Arabian Dhow. We depart from Forodhani garden on the seafront of Stone Town and sailing along the off-shores of the historical and fascinating coast while the sun sinks into the Indian Ocean starting to paint the town with its pastel amazing colors, tourists are taking the sea view passing around house of wonders, palace museum, old dispensary, custom house etc. As the sun sets, you will experience the most peaceful feeling. With just the sound of the water splashing beneath you, enjoy our open bar inclusive of cold soft drinks, beer & wine served along with Zanzibari snacks.

DAY 5 - BREAKFAST AND THEN TRANSFER TO AIRPORT FOR DEPARTURE
5 days Tanznia zanzibar safari tour

Breakfast, free time and then transfer to Airport.
